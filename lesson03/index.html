Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide users with information regarding the best way to adjust their daily lives to the ever-changing weather. It will provide information such as what the weather will be like throughout the day, what clothes are best to wear, what special gear you might need, what mode of transportation is best depending on where you're going, and which roads are best to take on route to your destiny.
